Company Profile

LAHAI Petroleum Service B.V is a Netherlands-registered international company specializing in comprehensive oil field services, civil engineering, logistics, procurement, HSE consulting, and telecommunications. Operating from our headquarters in Basra, Iraq, we serve major international oil companies across the Middle East and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, safe, and innovative energy services that exceed client expectations while maintaining the highest standards of health, safety, and environmental stewardship across all operations.

Our Vision

To become the leading integrated oil field services provider in the Middle East and North Africa, recognized for operational excellence, technological innovation, and unwavering commitment to HSE standards.

Civil Engineering

Procurement

Telecommunication
